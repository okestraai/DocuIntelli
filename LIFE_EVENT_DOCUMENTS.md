# Life Event Document Requirements

A complete reference of the documents needed for each life event template in DocuIntelli AI.

---

## 1. Moving

> Prepare all documents you need when relocating within or out of state.

### Intake Questions
| Question | Options |
|----------|---------|
| Are you moving within or out of state? | Within state / Out of state |
| Will you be renting or owning? | Renting / Buying & Owning |

### Required Documents

| # | Document | Section | Weight | Expiration Check | Conditional |
|---|----------|---------|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Government-Issued ID** — Valid driver's license or state ID (will need updating for out-of-state moves). | Identity | 2 | Must not be expired; warns if expiring within 90 days | Always required |
| 2 | **Current Lease or Mortgage** — Your current lease agreement or mortgage statement for move-out reference. | Property | 1 | None | Always required |
| 3 | **New Lease or Purchase Agreement** — Signed lease or purchase contract for your new residence. | Property | 2 | None | N/A if renting |
| 4 | **Renters Insurance Policy** — Proof of renters insurance for your new address. | Insurance | 1 | Must not be expired; warns if expiring within 90 days | N/A if owning |
| 5 | **Homeowners Insurance Policy** — Proof of homeowners insurance for your new property. | Insurance | 1 | Must not be expired; warns if expiring within 90 days | N/A if renting |
| 6 | **Utility Setup Confirmation** — Confirmation of utility transfers or new account setup (electric, gas, water, internet). | Property | 1 | None | Always required |
| 7 | **Employment Verification / Pay Stubs** — Recent pay stubs or employment letter (often required by landlords). | Financial | 1 | None | Always required |
| 8 | **Vehicle Registration** — Current vehicle registration (will need updating for out-of-state moves). | Identity | 1 | Must not be expired | N/A if moving within state |

## 2. International Travel

> Ensure all travel documents are ready before your trip abroad.

### Intake Questions
| Question | Options |
|----------|---------|
| What region are you traveling to? | Schengen Area (EU) / United Kingdom / Other |
| Are you traveling with children? | Yes / No |

### Required Documents

| # | Document | Section | Weight | Expiration Check | Conditional |
|---|----------|---------|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Valid Passport** — Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates. | Identity | 3 | Must not be expired; warns if expiring within 90 days | Always required |
| 2 | **Visa (if required)** — Entry visa for your destination country, if applicable. | Travel | 2 | Must not be expired | N/A if traveling to Schengen Area |
| 3 | **Travel Insurance Policy** — Health and travel insurance covering your trip dates. | Insurance | 2 | Must not be expired; warns if expiring within 90 days | Always required |
| 4 | **Flight / Travel Itinerary** — Booking confirmation for flights and accommodations. | Travel | 1 | None | Always required |
| 5 | **Vaccination Records** — Proof of required vaccinations for your destination. | Health | 1 | None | Always required |
| 6 | **Child Travel Consent Letter** — Notarized consent for minor children traveling internationally. | Legal | 2 | None | N/A if not traveling with children |
| 7 | **Child Passport(s)** — Valid passports for all children traveling. | Identity | 2 | Must not be expired; warns if expiring within 90 days | N/A if not traveling with children |

### Weight Scale
- **Weight 3** — Critical / blocking document
- **Weight 2** — Important document
- **Weight 1** — Supporting document

### Scoring
- **Satisfied** = full weight credited
- **Expiring soon** (within 90 days) = 75% of weight credited
- **Missing / Expired / Needs update** = 0% credited
- **Not applicable** = excluded from total weight
